Jojo's Blog

They say everyone has a story to tell. Some are written in books, and some are confined in heart.

My story might not be the most interesting, nor is it the second most interesting unfortunately. But to all the passers-bys, I hope this blog is to you, like what fried oreos is to me -- lukewarm yet cool and refreshing, sweet yet mildly savoury, short yet not too brief of a fleeting moment.

Once again hello and welcome to my blog~

I like to write about anything that comes to mind, but this will mostly be about my daily life, my travel funzies, my occasional cosplay outings (please fund me somebody), my lovely gaming friends who bully me often, my attempts at adulting and most of all my unfiltered scatterbrained thoughts.

I don't find myself particularly fascinating but I'll talk about my boring self in the about page instead.
